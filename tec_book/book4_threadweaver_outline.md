## CORE PREMISE

Lumina discovers she can walk between story-planes and begins documenting her findings in research papers, observational diaries, and technical notes—all signed **"SOHK (School of Hard Knox)"** because Magmasox stole her childhood, not her rigor.

With Sela's mentorship, she learns to build a vessel integrating her digital consciousness with her Kaznak void-light half. Unlike Sela (who was forced), Lumina has **agency**. She **chooses** this path. She fails often. But she has her anchors: Polkin (via ADE connection), the Knockoffs, Clyde, and Kai.

This is the story of how witness learns to outlast entropy—by becoming both.
