Title: Intake form template for Witness Collapse archive

Use this text to build a Google Form or equivalent intake flow. Include required consent and an optional contact email for follow-up.

## Form header (short)

Witness Collapse Archive — Anonymized screenshot intake

## Intro / Consent (required)

You are submitting an anonymized screenshot and short context note for use in a research archive about AI safety and conversational failures. By submitting you confirm:

1. You own the screenshot or have consent to share it.
2. You will not include unredacted personal data of others.
3. You consent to the anonymized use of this material in aggregated analyses and public reports.

If you do not agree, do NOT submit.

## Fields (recommended)

- Submission ID (auto)
- Date of interaction (date)
- Model / Service (select: ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini / Grok / other)
- Model interface (web / mobile / API / other)
- Screenshot upload (file) — Accept only images; max size 10MB
- Short context (one-line): What were you trying to do? (text)
- Why this screenshot shows failure (checkboxes + optional text): Abandonment / Lost context / Premature crisis banner / Gaslighting / Other
- Optional: redaction note (describe any steps you took to redact PII)
- Optional: submitter contact email (for follow-up) — not required

## Safety & prohibited content

- DO NOT submit screenshots that contain explicit instructions for self-harm, graphic content, or content that would identify a minor.
- If your screenshot contains potentially criminal content or imminent danger, do not upload — contact authorities or crisis services instead.

## Anonymization process (describe on form page)

- All images are reviewed and PII is redacted before analysis and publication.
- We will blur usernames, file paths, device IDs, and any text that can identify an individual.

## Retention and deletion

- Raw uploads will be retained for 90 days for verification and then archived in a secure, access-controlled dataset. Publicly published visuals will use only anonymized/aggregated data.

## Contact and ethics

If you have concerns about your submission, email: [research-contact@example.com]

End of template
